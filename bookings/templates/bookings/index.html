About Us

13 Years of experience in digital device repair services

At Restore by Klickit, we are your trusted laptop repair and desktop service center in India, specializing in laptops, desktops, and speaker repairs across all major brands. Whether it’s Dell, HP, Lenovo, Acer, Asus, Apple, or custom-built systems, our expert technicians provide fast, reliable, and affordable repair solutions.

With years of experience, we don’t just fix devices—we restore performance, productivity, and peace of mind. From motherboard repairs, screen replacements, and software troubleshooting to speaker repair and hardware upgrades, we handle every issue with precision and care.
